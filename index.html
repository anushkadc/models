Oily Florals

"Oily Florals" is a modern take on floral art, marked by thick, textured brushstrokes that give the work a sculptural, tactile quality. Vibrant, saturated colors—balanced with soft pastels and creamy whites—create dynamic contrasts that heighten visual and emotional impact. Blending realism with abstraction, this style captures both the natural beauty of flowers and the artist's personal expression. It transforms traditional floral painting into something bold and contemporary, celebrating nature while pushing creative boundaries.
